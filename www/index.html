Don’t let your food rot.

GardenHub picks your crops and gives them to people who need them.

How it Works

GardenHub takes your extra food and donates it to local charities and restaurants, depending on the need.

1.

Get Invited

Your garden manager will add your plot to GardenHub and invite you to use the app.

2.

Place an Order

You can manage your plot and place an order through the app. Cancel anytime.

3.

Kick Back!

Your crops get picked and donated, while you do whatever you want to instead.
